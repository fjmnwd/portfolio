Canton is a coastal proince in Southern China, with a population of more than 110 million people. Since the region is near to tropical, the ingredients in Canton provinve are extremely various. As for cuisines, Cantonese prefer to maintain the original freshness of the ingredients and create well-balanced and mild flavors.



Cookies

People in Guangdong province live a delicate life. In the morning, various Canton-style cookies guarantee them with energy.

Siu Mei

Siu mei is the generic Cantonese name of meats roasted on spits over an open fire or a large wood-burning rotisserie oven (from wiki).

Wonton noodles

The dish is usually egg noodles served in a hot broth, garnished with leafy vegetables and wonton dumplings. The types of leafy vegetables used are usually gai-lan, also known as Chinese broccoli or Chinese kale (from wiki).
