Candied Sweet Potatoes

The cuisine is made by wrapping sweet potatoes a layer of melting sugar, and frying them. The candied sweet potatoes remains tender and soft insides while flacky outsides.

Sweet & Sour Fish

As the name indicates, the cuisine is sweet and sour due to the carefully prepared sauce. Besides, the fish is fried into a shape of arch.

Sautéed Duck Gizzard and Pork Tripe

Duck gizzard and pork tripe are two things considered as crisp especially after sautéed. Traditonally, the cook may spend 1 hour or more to remove the inner membrane of gizzard for it to be more crisp. Therefore, it is extremly time-consuming, limiting its popularity.
