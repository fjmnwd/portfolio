Sichuan is an inland proince in Southwestern China, with a population of more than 80 million people. Its central part is within a huge basin surrounded by high mountains, resulting in a warm damp climate. The wet weather encouraged people to develope spicy dishes, which are believed to keep moisture from body.

Mapo Tofu

Mapo means a pock-marked grandma in Chinese, and by word of mouth, this cuisine was created by her. Combined with chili broad bean paste from Sichuan's Pixian county, the tofu owns a complex flavor, which is numbing, spicy hot, physically hot, fresh, tender, aromatic and flaky.

Sliced Beef and Ox Tongue in Chilli Sauce

This cuisine has a scaring Chinese name "husband and wife lung pieces". That was a joke played by michievous children on sliced beef made by a married couple. Its taste is again spicy and mouth-numbing, which is perfect to serve as an appetizer.
